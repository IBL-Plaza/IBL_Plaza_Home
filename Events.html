When [Healing] becomes [Virtual] Art Exhibiton

DATE: June 4 ~ June 23, 2022

Two artists from different places are trying to tell a story by painting during the pandemic, healing the people through arts, we might not have much chance to see the actual pieces, but we can see them in Decentraland where they connected the peoples.

Wei Ho, entrepreneur and educator by day, artist by night. He is an artist with an engineering background; he always believes art is just like an experiment; the result has to be made for a purpose. During the pandemic, he is making art with an A.I model he developed to draw like him, but add some "cyber emotion" to make it close to human.

Hugo Huang resigned from a tedious office job and became a full-time artist in Taipei in 2021. He demonstrated his determination and braveness by flying from Taipei to Germany at the end of 2021 and going on an adventure during the global pandemic. He aims to express an interpretation of a human being’s inner perception and their relationships with other individuals through arts.
